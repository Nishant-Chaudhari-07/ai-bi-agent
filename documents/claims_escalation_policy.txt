INTERNAL CLAIMS ESCALATION POLICY
Commercial Auto Insurance Division
Effective Policy Reference: Section 4.2 - Claims Authority Limits

1. CLAIMS HANDLER AUTHORITY LIMITS

Claims handlers are authorized to approve and settle commercial auto claims up to 
$50,000 without additional review. This authority applies to claims arising under 
the Business Auto Coverage Form (CA 00 01) for all covered auto designations.

2. SUPERVISOR APPROVAL THRESHOLD

Any commercial auto claim with a total claim amount exceeding $50,000 requires 
mandatory supervisor review and approval prior to settlement. This threshold 
applies regardless of incident type, including single vehicle collision, 
multi-vehicle collision, and vehicle theft claims.

3. SENIOR MANAGEMENT ESCALATION

Claims exceeding $75,000 require senior claims manager approval in addition to 
supervisor sign-off. Claims exceeding $100,000 require regional claims director 
notification within 24 hours of identification.

4. RATIONALE

This tiered approval structure ensures appropriate oversight scales with financial 
exposure, consistent with NAIC Unfair Claims Settlement Practices Act standards 
for reasonable claims investigation and fair settlement practices.

5. REVIEW CYCLE

This policy is reviewed annually and aligned with enterprise risk management 
guidelines. Last reviewed: Q1 2026.
